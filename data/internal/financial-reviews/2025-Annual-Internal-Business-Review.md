## 1. Executive Summary

**Fiscal Year 2025 in Review: Transformation Under Pressure**

Nexlify Corp delivered **$5.3B in revenue** for FY2025, representing **23% year-over-year growth** but falling short of our internal target of $5.8B by 8.6%. The shortfall was primarily driven by advanced packaging constraints and intensifying competitive pressure from NVIDIA's Blackwell launch.

**Key Achievements:**
- Launched NEXL-X3 inference accelerator (4.2M units shipped)
- Secured $420M in automotive contracts (BMW, Toyota)
- Expanded Europe/SEA market presence (+47% revenue)
- Achieved ISO 26262 ASIL-D certification for automotive line
- Reduced China revenue concentration to 14% (from 22%)

**Key Challenges:**
- Supply chain constraints limited revenue by ~$340M
- Gross margin compression to 49% (target: 52%)
- AI engineer attrition elevated at 16% annual
- Three significant customer delivery delays (Microsoft, Amazon, Meta)

**Strategic Outlook 2026:**
- Target: $6.8B revenue (+28% growth)
- NEXL-X4 launch (Q1 2026)
- Full automotive production ramp
- Cost restructuring to restore margin to 52%

### 3.2 NEXL-X3 Performance Analysis

**Strengths:**
- Lead in inference performance-per-watt (3.2 FLOPS/W vs NVIDIA B200's projected advantage)
- Strong enterprise customer adoption (1,200+ deployments)
- Software ecosystem maturing (PyTorch 2.4, TensorFlow 2.17 support)

**Weaknesses:**
- Memory bandwidth gap vs Blackwell (-65% vs B200)
- Limited software optimization vs NVIDIA CUDA ecosystem
- Lead times extended to 26 weeks (vs 16 week target)

**Customer Feedback Themes:**
- "Best inference value in market" — 72% positive
- "Software needs improvement" — 45% request
- "Delivery delays frustrating" — 38% complaint
