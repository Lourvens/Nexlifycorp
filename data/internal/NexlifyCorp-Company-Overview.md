## 1. What is Nexlify Corp?

Nexlify Corp is an AI infrastructure company headquartered in Austin, Texas, that designs and manufactures advanced semiconductors and computing platforms for artificial intelligence workloads. Founded in 2019, Nexlify has grown to become a leading provider of AI inference accelerators and edge AI solutions, serving hyperscale data centers, automotive manufacturers, and enterprise customers worldwide.

Nexlify's products power some of the most demanding AI applications, including data center inference, edge computing, autonomous driving, and large language model deployments.
