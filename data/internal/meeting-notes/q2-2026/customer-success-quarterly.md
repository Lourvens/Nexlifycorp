## 7. Customer Success Stories

### 7.1 Win: Walmart Smart Cart

**Situation:** Walmart deploying NEXL-E1 for smart cart in 500 stores

**Challenge:**
- Edge AI at scale (500 stores, 50K devices)
- Real-time inventory management
- 99.9% uptime requirement

**Solution:**
- Dedicated CSM (Maria Santos)
- Weekly calls with engineering
- 24/7 support during rollout
- Quarterly business review

**Result:**
- 99.7% uptime achieved
- 15% reduction in out-of-stock incidents
- $18M contract expanded to $35M

### 7.2 Win: GE Healthcare Medical Imaging

**Situation:** GE using NEXL-E1 for point-of-care ultrasound

**Challenge:**
- Medical device certification (FDA)
- Strict latency requirements (<10ms)
- Global deployment (40 countries)

**Solution:**
- Joint validation testing
- Regulatory documentation support
- Multi-region support infrastructure
- Quarterly compliance reviews

**Result:**
- FDA 510(k) cleared
- 500 units deployed
- $12M contract (potential $40M by 2028)

### 7.3 Win: Fanuc Factory Automation

**Situation:** Fanuc using NEXL-E1 for industrial robotics

**Challenge:**
- Factory environment (heat, vibration)
- Real-time control (<5ms)
- 10-year product lifecycle

**Solution:**
- Custom thermal testing
- Long-term support commitment
- Co-development of.robotics SDK

**Result:**
