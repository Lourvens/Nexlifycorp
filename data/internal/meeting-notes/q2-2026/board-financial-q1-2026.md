## 3. CEO Strategic Remarks — Michael Richardson

### 3.1 Q1 2026 Context

Michael opened with context on the post-breach quarter:

> "Q1 2026 tested our resilience. We faced two significant challenges: the aftermath of November's cybersecurity incident and continued supply chain pressures. Despite this, we delivered $1.38B in revenue within guidance and made substantial progress on security transformation. Our NEXL-X4 development remains on track for June tape-out."

### 3.2 Strategic Priorities Update

**Accomplishments:**
- Security breach contained and customer notifications completed
- NEXL-X4 RTL complete, tape-out on schedule
- Attrition reduced from 16% to 13%
- Europe revenue growth of 47% YoY continued
- BMW qualification on track for Q2 sample approval

**Challenges:**
- CoWoS lead times extended (24 weeks vs target 18 weeks)
- Automotive revenue below target ($38M vs $45M)
- Insurance recovery delayed (expected Q2)
- Legal costs elevated ($15M vs $10M budget)

### 3.3 NEXL-X4 Launch Acceleration

**Board Request:** Approve acceleration of NEXL-X4 production from January 2027 to December 2026

**Rationale:**
- Reduce IP exposure window post-breach
- Meet customer demand (Amazon, Google evaluation)
- Competitive response to AMD MI350

**Decision:** APPROVED UNANIMOUSLY
