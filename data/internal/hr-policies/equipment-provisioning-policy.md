# Nexlify Corp Equipment Provisioning Policy

**Version:** 2.9  
**Owner:** IT Operations / People Operations  
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-20  
**Classification:** Internal

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for the provisioning, use, maintenance, and return of company-provided equipment at Nexlify Corp. The goal is to ensure employees have the tools necessary to perform their jobs effectively while maintaining security and asset management standards.

## 2. Standard Equipment

### 2.1 Laptop

The standard laptop for all eligible employees is the **MacBook Pro 16" M3** with the following specifications:
- Processor: Apple M3 Pro chip
- Memory: 18GB unified memory
- Storage: 512GB SSD
- Display: 16.2-inch Liquid Retina XDR
- Keyboard: Full-size with Touch ID

**Included accessories:**
- 96W USB-C Power Adapter
- USB-C to MagSafe 3 Cable (2m)
- No additional peripherals included

### 2.2 Additional Equipment

The following equipment is available upon request (manager approval required):

| Equipment | Eligibility | Cost Limit |
|-----------|-------------|------------|
| External Monitor | All employees | $500 |
| Docking Station | Remote/hybrid employees | $300 |
| Keyboard (external) | All employees | $150 |
| Mouse | All employees | $100 |
| Headset | All employees | $200 |
| Webcam | All employees | $150 |
| Standing Desk Converter | Remote employees | $400 |
| Monitor Arm | All employees | $200 |

### 2.3 Exceptions

Equipment requests that exceed standard specifications or cost limits require:
- Manager written approval
- IT Operations review
- Department Head approval for amounts over $1,000

Non-standard equipment requests are evaluated based on job requirements and business justification.

## 3. Home Office Stipend

### 3.1 Eligibility

Remote and hybrid employees are eligible for a **one-time home office stipend of $1,500** to purchase furniture and equipment for their home workspace.

### 3.2 Eligible Purchases

The home office stipend may be used for:
- Ergonomic chair
- Standing desk or desk converter
- Additional monitor
- Desk and shelving
- Proper lighting
- Cable management solutions
- Office organization supplies

### 3.3 Submission Process

1. Purchase items and obtain receipts
2. Submit expense report in **Expensify** within 60 days of purchase
3. Select category "Home Office Equipment"
4. Attach itemized receipts
5. Manager approves in Expensify workflow

Receipts are required for all purchases exceeding $25. The stipend is not taxable income when used for qualifying office equipment.

### 3.4 Unused Funds

Unused home office stipend funds cannot be carried over or cashed out. The stipend is a one-time benefit; employees who leave and are rehired are not entitled to an additional stipend unless significant time has passed (1+ year) and circumstances have changed substantially.

## 4. Internet Reimbursement

### 4.1 Remote Employee Eligibility

All remote employees are eligible for monthly internet reimbursement up to **$75/month**.

### 4.2 Eligible Expenses

- Home internet service fees (basic tier or higher)
- Mobile hotspot data plans (if primary internet connection)
- Upgrades required for work purposes (e.g., upgrading from 50Mbps to 100Mbps)

### 4.3 Submission Process

1. Submit monthly expense in **Expensify**
2. Select category "Internet Reimbursement"
3. Due by last business day of each month for the previous month's expense
4. Include internet service bill or payment confirmation

### 4.4 Documentation

For new reimbursement requests, provide:
- Internet service provider name
- Monthly cost
- Proof of service (first bill or statement)

## 5. Deployment and Equipment Delivery

### 5.1 Deployment Windows

For employees required to work on-site during specific windows, the deployment schedule is:
- **Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Hours:** 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM PST

Equipment needed for on-site work should be requested at least 2 weeks before the scheduled deployment date.

### 5.2 Equipment Delivery

- Standard shipping: 5-7 business days within continental US
- Expedited shipping: 2-3 business days (additional cost may apply)
- International shipping: 10-14 business days (varies by country)

IT Operations will provide tracking information via Slack when equipment ships.

## 6. Equipment Maintenance and Repair

### 6.1 Warranty

All company equipment is covered by manufacturer warranty:
- MacBook Pro: AppleCare+ (3 years standard)
- Apple accessories: 1 year standard
- Non-Apple equipment: Varies by manufacturer

### 6.2 Repair Process

1. Contact IT Support via Slack (#it-support) or email (it-support@nexlifycorp.com)
2. Describe the issue in detail
3. IT Support will determine if repair or replacement is needed
4. For on-site repair, equipment may be swapped for a loaner while work is completed
5. Do not attempt self-repair or use third-party repair services without prior IT approval

### 6.3 Damage and Loss

- **Accidental damage:** Covered under AppleCare+ if enrolled; otherwise employee may be responsible for repair costs if negligence is determined
- **Loss or theft:** Report immediately to IT Support and People Operations; police report required for theft
- **Negligence:** Employees may be responsible for repair or replacement costs at management discretion

## 7. Equipment Return

### 7.1 Offboarding

All company equipment must be returned during the offboarding process:
1. IT Operations will schedule equipment return or shipping 1 week before last day
2. All equipment (laptop, accessories, any peripherals) must be returned
3. Employee will receive confirmation when equipment is received
4. Failure to return equipment may result in payroll deduction

### 7.2 Transfer or Role Change

When an employee changes roles:
- Assess current equipment needs for new role
- Return equipment no longer needed
- IT Operations will provision new equipment as needed
- Manager must approve equipment changes

### 7.3 Shipping Returned Equipment

For remote employees:
1. IT Operations will send a prepaid shipping label via email
2. Pack equipment in original packaging or equivalent protective packaging
3. Drop off at authorized shipping location within 5 business days
4. Retain tracking number until confirmation of delivery

## 8. Equipment Standards and Security

### 8.1 Personal Use

Company equipment may be used for limited personal use (email, web browsing, etc.) as long as:
- Use is occasional and does not interfere with work
- No illegal or prohibited content is accessed
- Personal use does not compromise security
- Company policies (Code of Conduct, Acceptable Use) are followed

### 8.2 Security Requirements

All company equipment must have:
- **Okta** authentication enabled
- **Tailscale** VPN installed and active when working remotely
- FileVault encryption enabled (MacBook)
- Screen lock enabled (5-minute timeout)

IT Operations will configure security settings during provisioning. Employees must not disable or circumvent security controls.

### 8.3 Access Levels

Equipment access is governed by the employee's security clearance level. See the Security Clearance Levels Guide for details. Equipment used for work at higher clearance levels must meet the security requirements for that level.
