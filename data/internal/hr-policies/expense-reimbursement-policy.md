# Nexlify Corp Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Version:** 3.8  
**Owner:** Finance / People Operations  
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-10  
**Classification:** Internal

---

## 1. Overview

Nexlify Corp reimburses employees for reasonable, documented business expenses incurred in the performance of their job duties. All expenses must be submitted through **Expensify** with proper documentation within 30 days of the expense.

## 2. Expense Categories and Limits

### 2.1 Home Office and Equipment

#### One-Time Home Office Stipend
All eligible employees receive a **one-time home office stipend of $1,500** to set up a productive home workspace. This stipend covers:
- Desk and ergonomic chair
- Monitor (up to 2 monitors)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Webcam and headset
- Cables and adapters
- Standing desk converter

**Eligibility:** Full-time remote employees and employees who transitioned to remote work after receiving equipment.

**Process:**
- Submit purchases through **Expensify** within 60 days of eligibility
- Receipts required for all items over $25
- Pre-approval required for single purchases over $500
- Remaining stipend funds cannot be cashed out

#### Standard Laptop
The standard laptop provided to all employees is the **MacBook Pro 16" M3**. If this laptop is damaged, lost, or stolen due to employee negligence, replacement costs may be deducted from payroll. Contact IT Support immediately for equipment issues.

### 2.2 Internet Reimbursement

Remote employees are eligible for monthly internet reimbursement up to **$75/month**.

**Eligible expenses:**
- Home internet service fees
- Mobile hotspot plans (if primary internet)
- Upgrades required for work (e.g., higher speed tier)

**Submission:**
- Submit through **Expensify** monthly
- Due by last business day of each month
- Receipts required for initial setup or changes

### 2.3 Travel

#### Air Travel
- Economy class for flights under 6 hours
- Premium economy or business class for flights exceeding 6 hours, with manager pre-approval
- Book through company travel portal when available
- Use most economical route (direct flights preferred when cost difference is under 20%)

#### Ground Transportation
- Taxi/rideshare: Reimbursed for business travel
- Rental car: Compact class unless traveling with 3+ colleagues or equipment requires larger vehicle
- Personal vehicle: Reimbursed at current IRS rate per mile

#### Accommodation
- Standard: Up to $200/night in major metropolitan areas, $150/night elsewhere
- Business travel to high-cost cities (NYC, SF, London, etc.): Up to $300/night with justification
- Extended stays (14+ days): Employee may negotiate reduced rates; contact Finance for approval

#### Meals During Travel
- Breakfast: Up to $20
- Lunch: Up to $30
- Dinner: Up to $50
- In total, daily meal expenses should not exceed $100

### 2.4 Client Entertainment

- Business meals with clients: Up to $150/person (including tip)
- Client entertainment events: Up to $300/person with prior approval from manager
- Alcohol limited to $50 per person during business meals

### 2.5 Professional Development

- Training courses and certifications: Up to $2,000/year with manager approval
- Conference attendance: Up to $3,000/year with Department Head approval
- Books and subscriptions: Up to $500/year (submit via Expensify with receipt)

## 3. Expense Submission Process

### 3.1 Submission Timeline
All expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date. Expenses submitted after 90 days may be denied without exception.

### 3.2 Required Documentation
- Original itemized receipts (photos acceptable via Expensify mobile app)
- Business purpose description for any expense over $50
- Attendee list for group expenses (meals, events)
- Client name for client entertainment expenses
- Project code for project-specific expenses

### 3.3 Approval Workflow
1. Employee submits expense in **Expensify**
2. Manager approves (or rejects with explanation)
3. Finance reviews for policy compliance
4. Approved expenses are reimbursed in the next payroll cycle (bi-weekly)

### 3.4 Rejected Expenses
If an expense is rejected, the employee will receive notification with the reason. Common rejection reasons:
- Missing receipt
- Missing business justification
- Category exceeds policy limits
- Expense submitted after 90-day deadline

Employees may appeal rejected expenses to Finance within 10 business days of rejection notification.

## 4. Pre-Approval Requirements

The following expenses require written pre-approval before incurring:

| Expense Type | Approval Required |
|--------------|-------------------|
| Single equipment purchase > $500 | Manager |
| Travel > $3,000 total | Department Head |
| International travel | Department Head + Finance |
| Conference > $3,000 | Department Head |
| Client event > $1,000 | Manager |
| Professional certification > $1,000 | Manager |
| Client entertainment > $150/person | Manager |

## 5. Prohibited Expenses

The following expenses will not be reimbursed:
- Personal subscriptions (streaming services, etc.)
- Alcohol for personal consumption
- Tobacco products
- Clothing or personal grooming items
- Traffic tickets or parking violations
- Personal phone calls while traveling
- Entertainment not directly related to business (movies, spa, etc.)
- Gifts over $75 per person (company gift policy applies)
- Expenses incurred while on unauthorized leave

## 6. Corporate Card

Eligible employees may be issued a corporate credit card through **Workday** request. Corporate cards are subject to:
- Monthly spending limits (typically $2,500-$5,000)
- Quarterly audit by Finance
- Immediate revocation for policy violations

Corporate card holders remain responsible for providing documentation for all charges within 30 days.

## 7. International Travel

### 7.1 Pre-Approval
All international travel requires:
- Manager approval
- Department Head approval
- Finance notification

Submit international travel requests at least 4 weeks in advance.

### 7.2 Per Diem
For international travel, per diem rates apply based on destination. Consult Finance for current per diem rates for specific destinations. Per diem covers meals and incidentals; accommodation is reimbursed separately with receipts.

### 7.3 Currency
Expenses in foreign currency should be reported in the original currency with USD conversion noted. Use the exchange rate on the date of the transaction. Expensify supports automatic currency conversion.

## 8. Questions

Questions about this policy should be directed to Finance via **Slack** (#finance) or email at finance@nexlifycorp.com.
