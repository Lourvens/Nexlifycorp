## 1. Welcome to Nexlify Corp

Welcome! This guide will walk you through your first 90 days at Nexlify Corp. Our goal is to help you integrate smoothly into your team, understand our culture, and set you up for success in your role.

## 2. Before Your Start Date

### 2.1 Pre-Employment Tasks
Prior to your start date, you will receive emails from **Workday** with:
- Background check authorization
- Employment agreement signing
- Direct deposit setup
- Benefits enrollment instructions

All documents must be completed at least 3 business days before your start date.

### 2.2 Equipment
Your standard laptop (**MacBook Pro 16" M3**) will be shipped to your home address or available at your designated office location, depending on your work arrangement. Allow 5-7 business days for delivery within the continental US.

International shipments may require additional processing time. Contact IT Support if your equipment has not arrived by your start date.

## 3. Day One

### 3.1 Orientation Schedule
Your first day will include:

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 9:00 AM – 10:30 AM | Company Orientation (All new hires) |
| 10:30 AM – 11:30 AM | HR Systems Walkthrough (Workday, Expensify) |
| 11:30 AM – 12:30 PM | Lunch (Team-specific) |
| 1:00 PM – 2:30 PM | Security Training and Access Setup (Okta, Tailscale) |
| 2:30 PM – 4:00 PM | Team Introduction and Role-Specific Onboarding |

### 3.2 Access and Tools
By end of Day One, you should have access to:
- **Okta** – Identity management and SSO
- **Slack** – Primary communication platform
- **Linear** – Project tracking
- **Notion** – Company wiki and documentation
- **Workday** – HR self-service
- **Expensify** – Expense reporting

If you experience any access issues, contact IT Support via Slack (#it-support) immediately.

## 4. First Week

### 4.1 Core Training Requirements
All employees must complete the following mandatory training within their first 5 business days:

1. **Information Security Fundamentals** (45 minutes)
   - Covers password policy, data classification, and secure communication
   - Required quiz (passing score: 80%)

2. **Anti-Harassment and Discrimination Training** (60 minutes)
   - Required by law in most jurisdictions
   - Certificate of completion generated in **Workday**

3. **Code of Conduct Acknowledgment**
   - Electronic signature in **Workday**

4. **Benefits Orientation**
   - Scheduled with People Operations
   - Covers health, dental, vision, 401(k), and other benefits

### 4.2 Role-Specific Onboarding
Your manager will provide:
- Introduction to team members and key stakeholders
- Overview of current projects in **Linear**
- Review of role-specific tools and processes
- Assignment of a buddy or mentor from your team

## 5. First 30 Days

### 5.1 Goals
By the end of your first month, you should:
- Complete all mandatory training
- Have 1:1 meetings scheduled with key colleagues and stakeholders
- Understand your team's current projects and your role in them
- Submit first expense report (if applicable)
- Begin contributing to team deliverables

### 5.2 Check-Ins
Your manager will schedule:
- Week 1 check-in (first Friday)
- Week 2 check-in
- Week 4 (30-day) review

These meetings are opportunities to ask questions, address concerns, and provide feedback on your onboarding experience.

### 5.3 Home Office Setup
If you are working remotely, your $1,500 home office stipend should be utilized within your first 60 days. All purchases must be submitted through **Expensify** with receipts. See the Equipment Provisioning Policy for eligible items.

## 6. First 60-90 Days

### 6.1 Expanded Responsibilities
As you become comfortable in your role, you should:
- Take on more independent projects
- Participate actively in team meetings and discussions
- Begin contributing to process improvements
- Build relationships across teams

### 6.2 60-Day Checkpoint
At 60 days, you and your manager will conduct a formal check-in to:
- Review progress against initial goals
- Identify any training or support needs
- Discuss feedback on the onboarding process

### 6.3 90-Day Review
At 90 days, a formal review will be conducted to:
- Evaluate role fit and performance
- Address any remaining onboarding gaps
- Confirm continued employment (if applicable based on role)

This review will be documented in **Workday**.

## 7. Remote Work Considerations

### 7.1 Deployment Windows
If your role requires on-site presence at any point, the deployment window is:
- **Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Hours:** 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM PST

Plan travel and accommodation accordingly when required to be on-site.

### 7.2 Internet Reimbursement
Remote employees are eligible for up to **$75/month** internet reimbursement. Submit expenses through **Expensify** by the last business day of each month.
