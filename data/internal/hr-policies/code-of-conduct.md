# Nexlify Corp Code of Conduct

**Version:** 4.1  
**Owner:** People Operations / Legal  
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-28  
**Classification:** Public

---

## 1. Introduction

Nexlify Corp is committed to fostering an inclusive, respectful, and professional environment for all employees, contractors, consultants, and guests. This Code of Conduct applies to all workplace interactions, whether in-person or virtual, including events, conferences, and business travel.

## 2. Our Values

### 2.1 Integrity
We act honestly and ethically in all business dealings. We do not tolerate fraud, misrepresentation, or dishonest practices of any kind.

### 2.2 Respect
We treat all individuals with dignity and respect, regardless of their role, background, identity, or opinion. We value diverse perspectives and constructive debate.

### 2.3 Excellence
We strive for high-quality work and continuous improvement. We take ownership of our responsibilities and deliver on commitments.

### 2.4 Collaboration
We work together across teams and functions to achieve shared goals. We share knowledge freely and support each other's growth.

### 2.5 Inclusion
We create an environment where everyone feels they belong. We actively work to eliminate bias, discrimination, and harassment in all forms.

## 3. Professional Conduct

### 3.1 Workplace Behavior
Employees are expected to:
- Communicate professionally in all interactions
- Arrive punctually for meetings and commitments
- Dress appropriately for their role and context (remote video calls included)
- Keep workspaces clean and organized (applicable to in-office and shared spaces)
- Use company resources responsibly and only for legitimate business purposes

### 3.2 Harassment and Discrimination
Nexlify Corp has zero tolerance for harassment and discrimination based on:
- Race, color, ethnicity, or national origin
- Gender, sex, or sexual orientation
- Age
- Religion
- Disability
- Genetic information
- Military or veteran status
- Any other protected class under applicable law

Prohibited behaviors include, but are not limited to: slurs, offensive jokes or images, intimidation, unwanted physical contact, and retaliation against anyone who reports a concern.

### 3.3 Substance Use
Employees should not work under the influence of alcohol, illegal drugs, or prescription medications that impair judgment. Exception: moderate alcohol consumption at company-sponsored events where legally permitted, and only for employees of legal drinking age.

## 4. Conflicts of Interest

### 4.1 Disclosure
Employees must disclose any situation that could create an actual or perceived conflict of interest, including:
- Outside employment or consulting
- Financial interests in competitors, vendors, or partners
- Personal relationships that influence business decisions
- Using company property or information for personal gain

Disclosures should be made to the employee's manager and People Operations in writing within 5 business days of becoming aware of the conflict.

### 4.2 Outside Employment
Employees may not engage in outside employment that conflicts with their Nexlify Corp responsibilities or uses company resources without prior written approval from People Operations and the employee's Department Head.

## 5. Confidentiality and Information Security

### 5.1 Confidential Information
Employees must protect confidential information, including:
- Proprietary technology and source code
- Business strategies and financial data
- Customer and employee personal information
- Internal communications and discussions

### 5.2 Access Levels
Employees are assigned security clearance levels that govern access to information. See the Security Clearance Levels Guide for details. Unauthorized access to information above one's clearance level is a serious violation.

### 5.3 Data Handling
All data handling must comply with the information security policies and applicable law. Personal data must be processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

## 6. Communication Standards

### 6.1 External Communication
Only authorized employees may speak on behalf of Nexlify Corp to media, analysts, or public audiences. All inquiries should be routed to the Communications team.

### 6.2 Social Media
Employees may discuss Nexlify Corp on personal social media but must not present themselves as official spokespeople. Content must not violate confidentiality obligations or be defamatory.

### 6.3 Internal Communication
All internal communication occurs through **Slack**, **Linear**, and **Notion**. Employees should use these tools professionally and refrain from sharing content that could be considered offensive or inappropriate.

## 7. Reporting Violations

### 7.1 Reporting Channels
Employees who witness or experience violations of this Code should report through one of the following:
- Direct supervisor
- People Operations (people-ops@nexlifycorp.com)
- Legal Department (legal@nexlifycorp.com)
- Anonymous Ethics Hotline (managed by third-party vendor)

### 7.2 Non-Retaliation
Nexlify Corp prohibits retaliation against anyone who makes a good-faith report of suspected misconduct or participates in an investigation. Retaliation is a separate violation of this Code.

### 7.3 Investigation Process
All reports will be reviewed promptly and, where appropriate, investigated confidentially. Employees found to have violated this Code may face disciplinary action up to and including termination.

## 8. Acknowledgment

All employees must acknowledge this Code of Conduct upon hire and annually thereafter through **Workday**. Managers are responsible for ensuring their teams complete acknowledgment.
