# Nexlify Corp Remote Work Policy

**Version:** 3.2  
**Owner:** People Operations  
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-15  
**Classification:** Internal

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for remote and hybrid work arrangements at Nexlify Corp. Our distributed-first culture enables employees to work effectively from home while maintaining collaboration, security, and work-life balance.

## 2. Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their onboarding period (typically 90 days) are eligible to request remote work arrangements. eligibility may be subject to role-specific requirements as determined by the employee's manager and People Operations.

## 3. Remote Work Options

### 3.1 Full Remote
Employees may work entirely from home or another approved location, subject to team collaboration requirements and manager approval.

### 3.2 Hybrid
Employees split time between home and an office location. Core collaboration days will be established by each team.

### 3.3 Occasional Remote
Employees primarily work on-site but may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

## 4. Home Office Requirements

### 4.1 Equipment
All remote employees receive a standard laptop: **MacBook Pro 16" M3**. Additional equipment must be approved by the employee's manager and must comply with information security requirements.

### 4.2 Home Office Stipend
Nexlify Corp provides a **one-time home office stipend of $1,500** to eligible remote employees. This stipend covers:
- Desk and chair
- Monitor and peripherals
- Webcam and audio equipment
- cables and adapters

The stipend is processed through **Expensify** within 30 days of the employee's start date or the date remote work is approved. Receipts are required for all purchases exceeding $25.

### 4.3 Internet Reimbursement
Remote employees are eligible for monthly internet reimbursement up to **$75/month**. Employees must submit expenses through **Expensify** by the last business day of each month.

## 5. Deployment Windows

For any role requiring on-site attendance or travel, deployment scheduling must adhere to the following windows:
- **Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Hours:** 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM PST

This window does not apply to fully remote positions unless explicitly stated in the employee's offer letter or role requirements.

## 6. Communication Expectations

### 6.1 Primary Communication Tool
All company communication occurs through **Slack**. Employees are expected to maintain active status during core hours (9 AM – 5 PM local time) and respond to messages within 2 business hours.

### 6.2 Meetings
Employees should use Google Meet or Zoom for video conferences. Cameras should be on during team meetings unless experiencing technical difficulties.

### 6.3 Documentation
All project tracking occurs in **Linear**. Employees must update task status at least once daily and document decisions in the appropriate project channels.

## 7. Security Requirements

Remote employees must comply with all security protocols, including:

### 7.1 VPN
All remote work must be conducted using **Tailscale** VPN. Connection is required when accessing any internal system or company data.

### 7.2 Identity Management
All employees must authenticate using **Okta** SSO. Password requirements and multi-factor authentication settings are managed through Okta.

### 7.3 Access Levels
Remote employees are assigned access levels based on their role. See the Security Clearance Levels Guide for details on access classifications.

## 8. Manager Responsibilities

Managers are responsible for:
- Ensuring team coverage during core collaboration hours
- Conducting regular check-ins (at minimum weekly 1:1s)
- Monitoring workload and preventing burnout
- Maintaining visibility into team productivity through **Linear**
- Ensuring at least one manager is on-site during any performance review quarter for in-person evaluations

## 9. Exceptions

Exceptions to this policy require written approval from the employee's Department Head and People Operations. Common exceptions include:
- Roles requiring physical presence (facilities, hardware engineering)
- Employees in their first 90 days (may have modified remote eligibility)
- Temporary accommodations for medical or personal circumstances

## 10. Policy Review

This policy is reviewed annually or upon significant changes to business needs. Questions should be directed to People Operations via **Slack** (#people-ops) or email at people-ops@nexlifycorp.com.
